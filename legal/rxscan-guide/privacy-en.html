Privacy Policy

RxScan Guide™
Legal version: 2026-07-10-v1
Effective date: 2026-07-10

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how RxScan Guide™ (the “Application”), developed and operated by RxScan Technologies Inc., handles information in its current MVP version.

2. MVP general principle

In its current MVP version, RxScan Guide™ operates without a user account and without Application-specific cloud storage. The Application is designed to help users understand a medication list through local tools and educational content.

3. Information processed in the Application

The Application may locally process images, OCR-extracted text, detected medication names, user selections, selected language and preferences needed for Application functionality.

This information may contain sensitive information if the user photographs or enters a medication list.

4. No account or profile

RxScan Guide™ does not require a user account in the current MVP. The Application does not create a named profile and does not intentionally request the user's name, address, health insurance number or medical identifiers.

5. Storage and transmission

In the current MVP, RxScan Guide™ does not provide Application-specific cloud storage for medication lists. Information used by the Application is processed within the device or usage session environment depending on the available features.

If the user voluntarily shares a screenshot, photo or information by email or another external service, that sharing is the user's choice and is governed by the rules of the service used.

6. OCR, matching and educational content

OCR and matching can be wrong. Extracted text, detected medication names and suggested cards must be verified against the original list or packaging.

The Application may provide general information and educational cards. It does not diagnose and must not be used to start, stop or change a treatment.

7. Technical data

Depending on the distribution platform and device settings, minimal technical data may be processed by device or app store systems, such as crash, compatibility or performance information. RxScan Technologies Inc. uses this information only to improve Application stability and quality when it is available.

8. Advertising and sale of data

RxScan Technologies Inc. does not sell user information. The current MVP of RxScan Guide™ is not designed to use medication lists for advertising purposes.

9. Security

Users should protect their device and avoid photographing or sharing sensitive information when it is not necessary. No information system can guarantee absolute security.

10. Rights and requests

For privacy questions or to request information about data processed by RxScan Guide™, users may contact RxScan Technologies Inc.

11. Changes

RxScan Technologies Inc. may update this Policy to reflect the evolution of RxScan Guide™. The applicable version is the version published on the official pages.
