Educational and Safety Warning

RxScan Guide™
Legal version: 2026-07-10-v1
Effective date: 2026-07-10

Important

RxScan Guide™ is an educational tool intended to help users understand a medication list. The Application is not a medical tool, does not diagnose and does not replace a healthcare professional.

No treatment decisions

No treatment should be started, stopped, interrupted, replaced or changed based on RxScan Guide™.

For any question about a medication, dose, interaction, side effect or treatment, consult a physician, pharmacist or other qualified healthcare professional.

OCR and matching

The Application may use OCR and matching to recognize or associate medication names. These technologies can be wrong.

  • a medication may be read incorrectly;
  • a medication may be confused with another;
  • a dose or form may be omitted or misinterpreted;
  • an educational card may be suggested incorrectly;
  • a result may be incomplete.

Mandatory verification

Users must verify all information against the original list, original packaging, pharmacy label or a healthcare professional.

General information

The Application may provide general information and educational cards. This information is not personalized and may not account for the user's age, weight, allergies, medical history, pregnancy, other medications or clinical situation.

No diagnosis

RxScan Guide™ does not diagnose, confirm any disease, rule out any medical condition or provide personalized medical advice.

Emergency

In a medical emergency, serious symptoms, allergic reaction, suspected overdose or significant uncertainty, immediately contact emergency services or a healthcare professional.
