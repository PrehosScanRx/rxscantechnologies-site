Privacy Policy

RxScan Paramedic
Legal Version: 1.0
Effective Date: June 17, 2026
Last Updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

RxScan Paramedic ("the Application") is developed and operated by RxScan Technologies Inc. and is committed to protecting the privacy and security of information processed through the use of the Application.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

2. Application Controller

The entity responsible for the Application and for information processed under this policy is RxScan Technologies Inc., located in Quebec, Canada.

3. Information Collected

The Application may collect certain technical information necessary for its operation, including:

  • application version;
  • accepted consent version;
  • selected language;
  • date and time of use;
  • application-generated technical identifiers;
  • error and diagnostic information.

4. Optional Information Submitted Through the Improvement Program

When users voluntarily participate in the RxScan Paramedic Improvement Program, certain information may be submitted to help improve the service, including:

  • analyzed photographs;
  • generated PDF reports;
  • OCR results;
  • detected medications;
  • analyses produced by the Application;
  • technical logs;
  • Scan IDs;
  • processing date and time.

Participation in this program is entirely optional.

5. Use of Information

Collected information may be used to:

  • improve medication recognition;
  • improve OCR performance;
  • correct software defects;
  • improve system stability;
  • develop new features;
  • analyze processing errors;
  • maintain service security and integrity.

Information is not used for advertising purposes.

6. Consent

Certain features rely on user consent.

Participation in the Improvement Program is entirely optional and may be declined without affecting normal use of the Application.

Accepted consent records may be stored locally for traceability purposes.

7. Data Retention

Information submitted through the Improvement Program may be retained as long as reasonably necessary for:

  • issue investigation;
  • product improvement;
  • validation of fixes;
  • legitimate development purposes.

Information may be deleted when it is no longer required.

8. Hosting and Security

Information may be stored using cloud services utilized by RxScan Technologies Inc. to operate the Application.

Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, disclosure, or modification.

However, no information system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Information Sharing

RxScan Technologies Inc. does not sell user information.

Information may only be shared:

  • with technical providers required to operate the service;
  • when required by law;
  • when necessary to protect legal rights and interests.

10. Withdrawal of Consent

Users may withdraw their consent to participate in the Improvement Program at any time when this functionality is available within the Application.

Withdrawal of consent does not affect processing already performed prior to withdrawal.

11. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of voluntarily submitted information by contacting RxScan Technologies Inc.

Certain information may be retained where required by law or necessary to protect legal rights.

12. Changes to This Policy

RxScan Technologies Inc. may modify this Privacy Policy at any time.

The version in effect will be the version published within the Application or on official documentation pages.
